Land's End to John O'Groats – May 2011

This May, a team of intrepid and foolhardy cyclists will set off from Land's End in Cornwall and position themselves gingerly onto a padded saddle for 11 days. Battling against the elements, long inclines, dodgy drivers and inner demons, we plan to cover the 1,000 miles to John O' Groats without suffering anything more serious than some mild chaffing…
Tell me more

When is it?

We start on Saturday 7th May and ride for 11 days, returning home on Wednesday 18th – we need to take 8 days off work but it will be worth it!

How far is it?

It's (gulp) 1012 miles, which is a leg-numbing 100+ miles per day – nothing for super-fit professional cyclists – but a bit of a challenge for mere mortals…
